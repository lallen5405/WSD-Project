About Us

TampaBay Jamz is the easiest way to keep your fingers on the pulse of Tampa's thriving local music scene. Wether you are a local band trying to get exposure, or new to the Tampa area and looking to get connected with great music, we can help. The best part? It's free!

TampaBay Jamz was created by Lee Allen, a Tampa Area musician turned web developer, to help assist some talented friends get more gigs. It has since evolved into Tampa's goto source for local music!
