About Us

Tampa's Local Music at Your Fingertips

TampaBay Jamz is the easiest way to keep your fingers on the pulse of Tampa's thriving local music scene. Wether you are a local band trying to get exposure, or new to the Tampa area and looking to get connected with great music, we can help. The best part? It's free!

Musicians

  1. Upload your Press Kit or create one for free!
  2. Get information for local venues to play your music!
  3. Publish photos from your shows for your fans to see!
  4. Connect with other musicians in the area to fill that empty spot!

Music Lovers

  1. See upcoming shows and venues in your area!
  2. Discover new great local music!
  3. Keep in touch with your favorite local musicians!
  4. Upload pictures of your favorite local bands to their fanpage!

TampaBay Jamz was created by Lee Allen, a Tampa Area musician turned web developer, to help assist some talented friends get more gigs. It has since evolved into Tampa's goto source for local music!

Sign-Up

It's easy, and it's FREE!

Signing up is easy, just fill out the form below to get started! Once you have signed up, you can create your profile, build your Press Kit and Fan Page, or you can just start browsing and posting to our forum. It's your site, you make it what you want it!

Be sure to sign up for our newsletter when you fill out the form. Our weekly newsletter gives you access to "inside information" including give aways from your favorite bands, and great information on other promotions availble in your area.
